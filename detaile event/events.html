EVENTS OF OWASP CLUB -PSNACET (2023-2024):

Club Inauguration:

  • Department of CSE (CYBER SECURITY) inaugurated a club titled “OWASP Club” on January 01, 2024 at Shannon Hall. The students of CSE(CS), ECE, CSE, IT, BME, and EEE attended the event.
  • Mr. Ashok kumar Mohan, Founder & CEO OWASP (Madurai) & Cyber Forensic Lead Consultant was the resource person. He discussed about the importance of cybersecurity and the evolving challenges in the field of cyber forensics, urging students to stay updated and actively engage in ethical hacking practices.

DIGITAL DEFENSE: EXPLORING KALI LINUX FOR NEWBIES- Hands on Workshop: (02-May-2024 to 03-May-2024)

Participants delved into the intricate functionalities of Kali Linux, exploring its robust suite of tools for cybersecurity and penetration testing. Through hands-on exercises and interactive sessions, participants gained practical skills in vulnerability assessment, ethical hacking techniques, and digital forensics. The workshop fostered a collaborative environment, allowing participants to exchange insights and problem-solving strategies.

Basics in the field of Hacking - Hands on Workshop: May 4th 2024

This event aims to fortify participants' knowledge in cybersecurity and foster a community of proactive digital defenders. Students were treated to engaging presentations and workshops covering a wide array of topics, including web security, threat intelligence, and secure coding practices. Through hands-on activities, participants forged connections and exchanged ideas, further enriching their understanding of cybersecurity best practices.

Intra Department Hackathon:

This event is organized to motivate the students to design, build & present the most innovative solution to a problem and develop prototype.

Cyber-Frontier -Capture the Flag (CTF) :

The event brought together talented minds from all around to showcase their cyber security skills, solve challenging puzzles, push the boundaries of ethical hacking and deepen their understanding in areas such as cryptography, web exploitation, and network security.

IOT Unplugged: Dive into Real-time Innovation:

The event aims to raise awareness about the importance of cybersecurity and equip participants with essential knowledge and skills to protect themselves in the digital world. The program covers a wide range of topics, including online safety, data protection, social engineering, and secure online practices.
